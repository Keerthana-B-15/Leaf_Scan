About Chili Leaf Disease Detection

Our advanced AI system helps farmers and gardeners identify diseases affecting chili plants through image analysis. Using deep learning, we provide accurate diagnoses and treatment recommendations to help maintain healthy crops.

Upload a clear image of the affected leaf to get instant results and recommendations for treatment. Our technology leverages a trained neural network model, capable of detecting six common chili diseases with high precision.

Key Features:

  • Real-Time Analysis: Get immediate insights with our fast-processing algorithm.
  • Visual Explanations: Grad-CAM technology highlights areas of the leaf that indicate disease, aiding in understanding the diagnosis.
  • Custom Recommendations: Tailored advice based on the identified disease to optimize crop health.
  • Accessibility: Available online and offline with a simple interface for all users.

Developed with cutting-edge research, this tool aims to reduce crop loss and improve agricultural productivity. Whether you're a small-scale gardener or a large-scale farmer, our system is designed to support your efforts in sustainable farming.

Stay informed with regular updates as we continue to enhance our model with new data and improved accuracy. Join us in revolutionizing chili farming with technology!

Common Chili Diseases

Bacterial Spot

Small, dark, water-soaked spots that eventually turn brown.

Curl Virus

Upward curling of leaves with yellowing and stunted growth.

Cercospora Leaf Spot

Circular spots with light centers and dark borders.

White Spot

White, powdery spots on leaf surfaces.

Nutrition Deficiency

Yellowing leaves, stunted growth, or discoloration due to lack of essential nutrients like nitrogen or potassium.
